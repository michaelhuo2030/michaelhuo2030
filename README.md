#### 🧭 What I'm thinking about

- **Hyperdimensional computing as a substrate**: not just retrieval — also streaming agent memory, compositional reasoning, and on-device AI under <0.1 W power budgets.
- **AI-augmented engineering**: AI + me + 1 part-time specialist = sustainable shipping. System judgment is the moat; AI is the executor; the specialist is the safety net.
- **Education as a 2-universe bridge**: the Problem-universe (what's worth doing) × the Knowledge / Abilities / Attributes-universe (what students grow). The bridge is 80% motivation, 20% scaffolding. Motivation is discovered, not commanded — through a careful journaling system that surfaces what each kid actually cares about. This is the seed of a PBL system I'm building with my wife for ages 2-18.
- **Tech transformation + Stiglitz's "Creating a Learning Society"** (Joseph Stiglitz, Nobel 2001) — the deepest driver of efficiency isn't capital, it's a society's collective rate of learning. Studying how tech transformations succeed when learning compounds, and fail when it stalls.
- **Living vs non-living**: what physical dynamics let a pattern self-sustain through the filter of cruel spatio-temporal nature? Where exactly is the boundary, and what does crossing it look like? Working at the intersection of HDC + neuromorphic + statistical physics here.
- **Open + honest pitches**: when my own benchmarks killed my old chip-thesis pitch, I [published the grave](TBD-medium-link). Reputation compounds; honesty compounds faster.
